Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: 6 May 2026 · Last updated: 6 May 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of personal data collected by Advora, including data obtained through Facebook and Instagram integrations.

How to request deletion

  1. Email privacy@advora.in from the email address linked to your Advora account.
  2. Use the subject line: Data deletion request.
  3. Include your Advora username and, if available, your Facebook user ID.

We confirm receipt within 2 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.

What data is deleted

  • Your Advora profile and account information.
  • Connected Meta access tokens.
  • Facebook Page and Instagram Business account data cached in Advora.
  • Posts, insights, analytics, schedules, and ad metrics linked to your account.

Alternative removal from Facebook

You may also revoke Advora access from Facebook by visiting Facebook Business Integrations, selecting Advora, and clicking Remove. To delete data already stored by Advora, please still send a deletion request using the email instructions above.

Contact

For privacy or deletion questions, contact privacy@advora.in.
